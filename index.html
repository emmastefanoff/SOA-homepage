Soul of Athens is an annual multimedia project produced by graduate and undergraduate students in the School of Visual Communication at Ohio University. Throughout one semester, the students are broken into smaller groups to collaborate with each other-journalists, photographers, web developers, editors, and designers-to develop multimedia stories.

Our Stories

This year's project features four interactive stories. Explore the life story of the Cincinnati Zoo's hippo, Fiona, two girls' experience of what it's like to be members of a boy scout troop, how cultural masculinity norms affects a man who seeks help for his mental health, and the tales, lore and mystique of Sasquatch.

It's Just Scouting

Girls are now allowed to join both the Cub Scouts and Scouts BSA programs as of February 2019. Although this decision was met with criticism, twins Mavery and Adele are excited to continue their lifelong journey in scouting.
